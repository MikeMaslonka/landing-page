About Lamborghini
Automobili Lamborghini is a legendary Italian brand known for creating some of the world’s most powerful and iconic supercars. Founded in 1963, Lamborghini has become a symbol of cutting-edge design, extreme performance, and bold innovation. From the roar of its engines to the unmistakable lines of its models, every Lamborghini is built to deliver an uncompromising driving experience.
